# EU Regulatory Overview for Medicinal Cannabis

## Objective
Provide a high-level overview of the European regulatory framework for medicinal cannabis, focusing on quality, safety and patient access.

## Regulatory Basis
- EU-GMP (EudraLex Volume 4)
- GACP for medicinal plants
- National competent authorities (e.g., BfArM, INFARMED)
- EMA guidance documents

## Key Regulatory Requirements
- Pharmaceutical-grade quality standards
- Traceability from cultivation to dispensing
- Valid Certificate of Analysis (COA)
- Stability and shelf-life data
- Pharmacovigilance obligations

## Clinical Relevance
Regulatory compliance ensures:
- Patient safety
- Consistent dosing
- Therapeutic reliability
- Legal market access across EU member states
